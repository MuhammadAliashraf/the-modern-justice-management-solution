# 🛡️ FirConnect — The Modern Justice Management Solution  
**Bridging the Gap Between Citizens and Law Enforcement with Digital Transparency.**

---

## 🌟 Overview  
In an era where every second counts, traditional, paper-based incident reporting is a bottleneck to justice. **FirConnect** is a cutting-edge FIR (First Information Report) Management System designed to digitize, streamline, and secure the legal reporting process.  

From the moment a citizen files a report to the final investigation log by an officer, FirConnect ensures **transparency, accountability, and speed.** It’s not just a database; it’s a workflow-driven ecosystem that empowers Station House Officers (SHOs) and Investigating Officers (IOs) to resolve cases with precision.

---

## 🚀 Key Features  

### 🔒 Secure Role-Based Ecosystem  
Different dashboards for **Citizens, SHOs, IOs, and Administrators.** Every action is authenticated via JWT and secured by industry-standard encryption.  

### 📝 Seamless Digital Filing  
Citizens can report incidents from the safety of their homes. Detailed forms capture victim info, incident locations, demographics, and impact assessments accurately.  

### ⏳ Real-Time Case Tracking  
No more repeated visits to the station. Real-time status updates (Pending → Approved → Investigation → Closed) and automatic deadline tracking keep everyone informed.  

### 📊 Intelligent Workload Distribution  
SHOs can monitor officer performance and assign new FIRs to available Investigating Officers with a single click, ensuring no case is left behind.  

### 📑 Comprehensive Audit Logs  
Every change, every update, and every status shift is logged. A full historical trail ensures integrity and prevents unauthorized tampering.
